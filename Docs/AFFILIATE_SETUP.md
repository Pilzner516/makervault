# MakerVault — Affiliate Program Setup Guide

Step-by-step instructions for signing up to each affiliate program. Complete these to earn commission when MakerVault users click supplier links.

---

## 1. Amazon Associates (3–4% · 24hr cookie)

**Network:** Amazon Associates (direct)
**Signup:** https://affiliate-program.amazon.com

### Steps:
1. Go to https://affiliate-program.amazon.com and click "Join Now for Free"
2. Sign in with your Amazon account (or create one)
3. Fill in account information:
   - **Payee name:** Your legal name or business name
   - **Address:** Your address
4. Website/app details:
   - **Website:** `https://makervault.app` (or your domain)
   - **App name:** MakerVault
   - **Topics:** Electronics, Hardware, Tools, DIY, 3D Printing
5. Traffic & monetisation:
   - **How do you drive traffic?** Mobile app with product links
   - **How do you monetise?** Affiliate links within product search results
   - **Monthly visitors:** Start with "Under 500" if new
6. Create your Associate Tag:
   - Suggested: `makervault-20`
7. Complete tax information (W-9 for US)
8. Once approved, enter your tag in MakerVault Settings → Affiliate Codes → Amazon

**URL template:** `https://www.amazon.com/s?k={query}&tag={your-tag}`

## 2. MatterHackers via ShareASale (5% · 15-day cookie)

**Network:** ShareASale
**Signup:** https://www.shareasale.com/info/affiliate.cfm

### Steps:
1. Go to ShareASale and click "Affiliate Sign Up"
2. Create your account:
   - **Username:** makervault
   - **Website:** Your MakerVault domain or app store listing
   - **Country/Region:** United States
3. Complete the application:
   - **Primary promotional method:** Mobile application
   - **Website description:** "MakerVault is a mobile inventory app for electronics makers. We help users find and purchase components from trusted suppliers."
   - **Marketing channels:** Mobile app, in-app product links
4. Once approved, search for "MatterHackers" in the merchant directory
5. Apply to join the MatterHackers program
6. Get your affiliate link format from the program dashboard
7. Your affiliate ID will be in the format: `u=YOURID`

**URL template:** `https://www.shareasale.com/r.cfm?b=BANNER&u={affiliate_code}&m=MERCHANT&urllink=https://www.matterhackers.com/search?q={query}`

## Where to enter codes in MakerVault

1. Open MakerVault → Settings (gear icon on Home)
2. Scroll to "Affiliate Codes" section
3. Enter each code next to the matching supplier
4. Codes are saved to your account and applied to all supplier links automatically
5. When a user taps a supplier link, your affiliate code is appended to the URL

---

## Tips for approval

- **Be specific** about MakerVault in your applications — "parts inventory app for electronics makers"
- **Mention the user journey:** "Users scan parts with AI, search inventory, and tap supplier links to reorder"
- **Traffic source:** Always say "Mobile application" as primary
- **Revenue model:** "Affiliate commissions from product search links within the app"
- **Start with Amazon** — it's the easiest to get approved and has the broadest catalog
